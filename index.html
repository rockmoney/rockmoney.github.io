Monad Ecosystem — My Personal Opinion

The content below reflects my personal opinions, concerns, and experiences. It is not presented as factual findings, and readers should conduct their own independent research (DYOR) before making any financial, cryptocurrency, or investment decisions.

I have significant concerns regarding the Monad ecosystem, certain projects built around it, and the way some community and marketing efforts have been conducted. Based on my observations, I believe hype and promotion have often received more attention than transparency, accountability, and rewarding long-term community participation.

One of my biggest concerns involves airdrop allocation decisions. In my opinion, many early supporters spent substantial time testing products, participating in campaigns, promoting projects, and contributing feedback with the expectation that their efforts would be recognized.

I am also concerned about the long-term sustainability of projects that rely heavily on promotional campaigns and speculative activity. In my view, healthy ecosystems should prioritize transparency, user trust, product utility, and fair treatment of participants.

Readers should independently evaluate every project, team, token, exchange, wallet, NFT collection, and DeFi platform before participating.

This page is intended to share my personal perspective and encourage visitors to research all claims, review available evidence, and make informed decisions based on multiple sources of information rather than any single opinion.
